What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is digital money that operates on decentralized technology, allowing peer-to-peer transactions without banks. Bitcoin and Ethereum are the most well-known examples.

Protecting Yourself from Scams

Scammers use fake exchanges, phishing links, and social media tricks. Always double-check websites, never share private keys, and remember: if it sounds too good to be true, it probably is.

The Psychology of Money

Fear and greed dominate the market. Good traders learn to manage emotions and stick to a strategy, not the hype. Arnie will help you stay smart.
